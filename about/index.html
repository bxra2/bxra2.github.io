About

I got a Bachelor’s degree from the University of Trento, and my Master’s degree from the University of Milan. Currently, I work as a Software Engineer at Amazon Berlin.

During my studies, I gained a lot of interest in algorithms and data structures, as well as distributed systems, databases, and search engines. I explored those topics with my master’s thesis on graph compression, and with side projects on LSM tree and search engine implementations.

I had the opportunity of being a contestant at SWERC, a team competitive programming competition, in 2020 and 2022, also, I liked to participate in Google Hashcode every time I got the chance.

In my free time, I like to practice calisthenics and improve my cooking. I also read occasionally but not as much as I should.
